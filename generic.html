Hello and welcome to my data-driven wonderland!

My name is Karunya Munagala, and I come from the vibrant and culturally rich southern part of India, renowned for its delectable biryani. Yes, you guessed it right! I am from Hyderabad, a city that embraces both tradition and modernity.

Currently, I am pursuing my Master's in Applied Data Science, and I am incredibly enthusiastic about leveraging data to gain valuable insights and solve real-world challenges. As a data analyst, I am on a quest to uncover meaningful patterns and trends that can drive data-informed decisions.

Here, you will find a curated collection of projects showcasing my proficiency in data manipulation, visualization, and interpretation. From data cleaning and exploratory analysis to predictive modeling and data-driven decision-making, each project reflects my dedication to extracting valuable information from complex datasets.

In my pursuit of continuous learning, I have developed proficiency in several programming languages, such as Python, C, HTML, and R. Additionally, I am well-versed in essential data science tools like NumPy, SciPy, Pandas, scikit-learn, Matplotlib, Keras, and TensorFlow. These tools empower me to efficiently explore data, create predictive models, and visualize results effectively. I also frequently use SQL, PowerBI, and Tableau for my data analysis projects.

Throughout my academic journey and hands-on projects, I have developed a strong foundation in statistical analysis and data-driven problem-solving. My passion for data and its potential to make a positive impact drives me to seek summer internships in 2024, where I can apply my skills and contribute to data-driven initiatives.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I hope you enjoy exploring my data-driven world. If you have any inquiries or potential collaboration opportunities, Let's embrace the power of data together and make meaningful contributions in the world of analytics!
